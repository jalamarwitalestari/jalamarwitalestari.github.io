ICCoSIS 2026

International Conference on Computer Science and Intelligent Systems is A forum for researchers, practitioners, and students to share advances in AI, data science, software engineering, networks, security, and intelligent systems. Showcase your work, build collaborations, and publish high-quality proceedings. ICCoSIS focuses on impactful research and practical innovation. The conference welcomes original contributions, case studies, and system implementations.

Scope

  1. Computer science foundations
  2. Intelligent systems
  3. Aplied AI
  4. Cross-disciplinary computing

Publication

Proceedings and indexing follow the selected publisher policy. Provide camera-ready manuscripts with ethical compliance.

Audience

Researchers, lecturers, students, industry engineers, and policy makers in digital transformation.
